Bill Barrett Sr.

Bill Barrett Sr. is Licensed Real Estate Broker with over 30 years of experience in the real estate business. As the Broker of Record for Luxury Home Consultants, Bill focuses exclusively on the representation of luxury home Buyers purchasing estates or penthouses valued in excess of $1 million. An early advocate and practitioner of Exclusive Buyer Agency, Bill believes strongly in thorough Buyer Client representation. Through full disclosure, expert insight and collaborative negotiation Bill has found the formula for repeated success. This assures that his Clients get the best possible terms and lowest price for their luxury home purchase.
